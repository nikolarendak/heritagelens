Microfilm Digitization Project

Advanced ML Processing for Damaged Archives

Project Background

Our organization is a joint effort funded by Kodak Alaris, the US National Archives and Records Administration (NARA), The British Library, and the National Library of Australia to provide a specialized program for microfilm digitization. This initiative focuses particularly on damaged microfilm, aiming to recover, process, and extract valuable information from these fragile historical records.

Project Objectives

  • Digitize damaged microfilm from partner institutions
  • Apply advanced Machine Learning (ML) models to:
    • Enhance and restore image quality
    • Recognize and extract text (from OCR)
    • Identify and classify document types
    • Extract contextual information and metadata
    • Detect and flag sensitive or confidential information
    • Create searchable, indexed digital archives with reference to original microfilm and raw scan
    • Ensure compliance with archival standards and data protection regulations
